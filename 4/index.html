Fringe news networks

Breitbart

Breitbart, though it isn't the most reputable source (for obvious reasons), plays a role in our current discourse. Founded by Andrew Breitbart, this outlet is known for providing reactionary talking points that are often parroted by more influential people. It is funded by Robert Mercer, who gave at least $10 million to the outlet. That's quite interesting, seeing as Robert Mercer has a vested financial interest in loosening government restrictions in the banking sector (something that Breitbart supports).
