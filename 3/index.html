Rachel Maddow

Maddow is known for being an MSNBC host that always kept talking about Russian Agents and the US election. She is often criticised for being conspiratorial. She is worth more than $20 million, and has an annual salary of $7 million

FOX

Tucker Carlson

Tucker Carlson is arguably one of the most influential Cable News presenters. His stepmother was a wealthy heiress to the Swanson frozen food empire. He is currently worth over $20 million, and has a salary of $6 million a year.

Laura Inghram

Laura Inghram is the host of the Inghram Angle, a popular news show on FOX. She is currently worth over $70 million with an annual salary of $15 million.

Judge Jeanine

Jeanine Pirro, host of FOX's Justice with Judge Jeanine. She is worth over $12million and has an annual salary of $3million.

The Five

The Five is a show on FOX news that comprises of Greg Guttfeld (worth over $5 million), Dana Perino (worth over $6 million), Jesse Watters (worth over $5million), and Juan Williams (worth over $2 million) These people have some of the dumbest takes on the planet, yet they are worth millions.
